# AINews9 Project

## Overview

AINews9 is India's AI Literacy Platform.

The platform is designed to help ordinary Indians understand, use and benefit from Artificial Intelligence through structured learning, practical guidance and responsible AI adoption.

---

# Mission

Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian.

---

# Vision

Build India's most trusted AI Literacy Platform helping people Learn AI, Use AI, Stay Safe and Grow with AI.

---

# Strategic Pillars

## Learn AI

Help people understand AI concepts from beginner to advanced.

---

## Use AI

Teach practical AI skills and tools for everyday life and work.

---

## Stay Safe

Promote responsible, ethical and secure use of AI.

---

## Grow with AI

Help people improve careers, businesses and productivity using AI.
