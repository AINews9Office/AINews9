# AINews9 - VC Deck Master Narrative

## Purpose

This document captures the core investor narrative, presentation structure, messaging and positioning behind the AINews9 VC Deck.

The deck may evolve over time, but the underlying story should remain consistent.

---

# Executive Summary

AINews9 is evolving from an AI News Website into India's AI Literacy Platform.

Mission:

Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian.

Vision:

Enable millions of Indians to confidently Learn AI, Use AI, Stay Safe and Grow with AI.

AINews9 is designed as an AI-native platform combining content, education, automation and AI assistance to accelerate AI literacy across Bharat.

---

# Core Investment Thesis

Most AI companies are building AI technology.

AINews9 is focused on helping people benefit from AI.

This creates an opportunity to become India's trusted AI literacy destination for students, teachers, parents, professionals, senior citizens and small businesses.
