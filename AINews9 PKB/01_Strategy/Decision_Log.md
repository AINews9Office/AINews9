# AINews9 - Decision Log

This document records all important strategic, product, technology, business and operational decisions made for AINews9.

---

## Decision 001

Date: June 2026

Decision:
AINews9 will evolve from an AI News Website into India's AI Literacy Platform.

Reason:
Long-term value lies in AI literacy, education, awareness and adoption rather than news aggregation alone.

Status:
Approved

---

## Decision 002

Date: June 2026

Decision:
The platform will focus on four strategic pillars.

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

Reason:
These pillars cover the complete AI journey for Indian users.

Status:
Approved

---

## Decision 003

Date: June 2026

Decision:
Primary target audiences will be:

* Students
* Teachers
* Parents
* Professionals
* Senior Citizens
* Small Businesses

Reason:
These represent India's largest AI adoption opportunities.

Status:
Approved

---

## Decision 004

Date: June 2026

Decision:
Continue using WordPress and Hostinger.

Reason:
Current infrastructure is stable, low cost and sufficient for Phase 1 and Phase 2 growth.

Status:
Approved

---

## Decision 005

Date: June 2026

Decision:
Create beta.ainews9.com before any major redesign.

Reason:
Allows experimentation without impacting production users.

Status:
Approved

---

## Decision 006

Date: June 2026

Decision:
Evaluate Blocksy as the future WordPress theme.

Reason:
Provides significantly more homepage flexibility than Provo News.

Status:
Under Evaluation

## Decision 007

Date: June 2026

Decision:
Use AI extensively across content creation, automation, publishing and product development.

Reason:

Status:
Approved

## Decision 008

Date: June 2026

Decision:
Homepage architecture should eventually be organized around strategic pillars rather than news categories.

Target Navigation:

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

Reason:

Status:
Approved

## Decision 009

Date: June 2026

Decision:
Adopt an AI-first operating model.

Human Responsibilities:

* Strategy
* Trust
* Quality
* Community

AI Responsibilities:

* Research
* Summarization
* Drafting
* Publishing Automation
* Content Operations

Status:
Approved

---

## Decision 010

Date: June 2026

Decision:
Build in phases rather than performing a full platform rewrite.

Roadmap:

Phase 1 → Awareness Platform

Phase 2 → AI Literacy Platform

Phase 3 → Learning Ecosystem

Phase 4 → AI Companion Platform

Reason:
Reduces risk and preserves momentum.

Status:
Approved

## Decision 011

Date: June 2026

Decision:
AINews9 Homepage v1 (Beta) is approved as the official homepage prototype for beta.ainews9.com.

Reason:
The design successfully represents the transition from an AI News Website to an AI Literacy Platform while aligning with the Learn AI, Use AI, Stay Safe and Grow with AI framework.

Status:
Approved
