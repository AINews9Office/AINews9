# AINews9 - Master Context

## Mission

Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian.

---

## Vision

AINews9 is evolving from an AI News Website into India's AI Literacy Platform.

The long-term objective is to empower every Indian to:

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

AINews9 aims to become the trusted destination for practical AI education, awareness and adoption across Bharat.
