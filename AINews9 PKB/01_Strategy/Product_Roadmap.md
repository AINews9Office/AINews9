# AINews9 - Product Roadmap

## Vision

Transform AINews9 from an AI News Website into India's most trusted AI Literacy Platform.

Mission:

Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian.

---

# Strategic Pillars

All future products, content and platform features should align with one or more of the following pillars:

## Learn AI

Help people understand AI.

## Use AI

Help people apply AI in daily life.

## Stay Safe

Help people use AI responsibly and securely.

## Grow with AI

Help people improve careers, businesses and opportunities through AI.
