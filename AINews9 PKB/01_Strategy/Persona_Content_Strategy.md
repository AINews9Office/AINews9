# Persona_Content_Strategy.md

Version: 1.0

Project: AINews9 – India's AI Literacy Platform

Mission: Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian

---

# Purpose

This document defines the target personas of AINews9 and the content strategy required to support each audience.

The objective is to ensure that all content, learning journeys, AI tools, Pragya Assistant recommendations and future personalization features are aligned with user needs.

---

# AINews9 Audience Framework

AINews9 serves six primary audience groups:

1. Students
2. Teachers
3. Parents
4. Professionals
5. Senior Citizens
6. Small Businesses

Each persona will eventually have:

* Dedicated Landing Page
* Learning Path
* Recommended Articles
* Recommended AI Tools
* Pragya Guidance
* AI Safety Resources
* Growth Resources
