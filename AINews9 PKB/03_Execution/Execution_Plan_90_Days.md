# AINews9 - Execution Plan (Next 90 Days)

## Objective

The objective of the next 90 days is NOT to build a large platform.

The objective is to validate the AI Literacy Platform vision while maintaining momentum on the existing AINews9 website.

Success will be measured by execution, learning and platform readiness rather than revenue.

---

# Success Criteria

At the end of 90 days:

* Beta website available
* Blocksy evaluated
* Homepage prototype built
* Audience journeys defined
* Content strategy established
* Pragya roadmap defined
* Newsletter foundation created
