# AINews9 - Website Architecture

## Purpose

This document defines the target website architecture for AINews9 as it evolves from an AI News Website into India's AI Literacy Platform.

The architecture should support:

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

while serving multiple audience segments.

---

# Current Architecture

Platform:

* WordPress

Hosting:

* Hostinger

Theme:

* Provo News

Primary Content:

* AI News
* AI Articles
* AI Quotes
* AI Career Content

Limitations:

* News-centric layout
* Limited homepage customization
* Audience journeys not clearly defined
* Learning content not organized by persona

---

# Future Architecture Principles

## Principle 1

Audience First

The platform should be organized around user needs rather than content types.

---

## Principle 2

Learning First

The platform should help users learn and apply AI rather than only consume news.

---

## Principle 3

AI Native

Automation should be built into content creation, publishing and user support.

---

## Principle 4

Simple for Bharat

Content should remain easy to understand and practical.
