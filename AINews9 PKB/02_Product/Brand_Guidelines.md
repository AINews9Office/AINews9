# AINews9 - Brand Guidelines

## Purpose

This document defines the official branding, visual identity and communication standards for AINews9.

It serves as the single source of truth for presentations, website design, marketing material, social media content and future product development.

---

# Brand Identity

## Brand Name

AINews9

---

## Tagline

Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian.

---

## Mission

Help millions of Indians confidently understand, adopt and benefit from Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Vision

Transform AINews9 from an AI News Website into India's most trusted AI Literacy Platform.

---

# Brand Positioning

AINews9 is not merely an AI news portal.

AINews9 is:

* An AI Literacy Platform
* An AI Awareness Platform
* An AI Adoption Platform
* A Trusted AI Guide for Bharat

---

# Brand Personality

AINews9 should feel:

## Trustworthy

Reliable and responsible.

---

## Practical

Focused on real-world value.

---

## Educational

Simple explanations over technical jargon.

---

## Inclusive

Designed for all Indians regardless of technical background.

---

## Future Ready

Forward-looking and innovation focused.

---

# Strategic Pillars

All branding and messaging should reinforce:

## Learn AI

Understand AI concepts and technologies.

## Use AI

Apply AI in daily life and work.

## Stay Safe

Use AI responsibly and securely.

## Grow with AI

Improve careers, businesses and opportunities.

# Typography Principles

Typography should be:

* Clean
* Modern
* Easy to read
* Mobile friendly

Avoid:

* Decorative fonts
* Overly technical styling
* Excessive visual complexity

---

# Presentation Standards

All future presentations should follow:

## Layout

* 16:9 format
* Clean spacing
* Consistent alignment

## Header

AINews9 branding should appear consistently.
