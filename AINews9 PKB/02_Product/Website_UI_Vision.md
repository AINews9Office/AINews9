# AINews9 - Website UI Vision

## Purpose

This document captures the long-term user experience and website vision for AINews9.

The goal is to evolve AINews9 from an AI News Website into India's AI Literacy Platform while maintaining a simple, intuitive and scalable user experience.

This document serves as the design north star for all future website evolution.

---

# Vision Statement

AINews9 should become the simplest place for Indians to:

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

The website should feel:

* Simple
* Trustworthy
* Helpful
* Educational
* Practical
* AI Native

---

# Theme Evolution Strategy

Current Theme:

Provo News

Future Theme Strategy:

Blocksy (Selected)

Rationale:

Selected as the preferred future theme because it provides:

* Flexible homepage design
* Landing page capabilities
* Audience journey support
* Better AI literacy platform experience
* Scalability beyond a traditional news portal

Provo remains the production theme until the Blocksy-based beta environment is validated.

---
