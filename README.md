# About AINews9

AINews9 is evolving from an AI News Website into **India's AI Literacy Platform**.

Our mission is to help every Indian confidently understand, use and benefit from Artificial Intelligence through simple, practical and trustworthy learning experiences.

Rather than focusing only on AI news, AINews9 is building a complete AI learning ecosystem powered by human expertise and AI.

---

Build India's most trusted AI Literacy Platform helping people:

- Learn AI
- Use AI
- Stay Safe
- Grow with AI

# Core Products

## 🌐 AINews9 Website

The primary AI Literacy Platform delivering:

- AI Learning
- AI News
- Educational Guides
- Persona-based Learning
- Practical AI Resources

---

## 🤖 Pragya AI Learning Assistant

Pragya is the flagship AI product.

Pragya is **not** a general-purpose chatbot.

Its purpose is to:

- Guide AI learning
- Explain AI concepts
- Recommend relevant articles
- Personalize learning journeys
- Improve AI literacy

# Engineering Principles

Always:

- Preserve architecture.
- Keep code modular.
- Reuse existing services.
- Write production-ready code.
- Keep documentation synchronized.

Never:

- Hardcode secrets.
- Break architecture.
- Duplicate business logic.
- Rewrite working modules unnecessarily.
