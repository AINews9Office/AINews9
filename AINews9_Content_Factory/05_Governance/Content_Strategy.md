# AINews9 Content Strategy

Version: 1.0

Status: Strategic Document

Last Updated: June 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines how AINews9 creates, manages, distributes, repurposes, and scales content.

It translates the Editorial Constitution into an executable content system.

---

# Strategic Objective

Transform AINews9 from an AI News Website into India's AI Literacy Platform.

Content is the primary vehicle for achieving this transformation.

---

# Content Mission

Help people:

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

Every content initiative should support one or more of these outcomes.

---

# Content Ecosystem

AINews9 content consists of:

## 1. Cornerstone Content

Long-form evergreen content.

Examples:

* What is AI?
* What is ChatGPT?
* What are AI Agents?

Purpose:

Build long-term authority.

Priority:

Highest

---

## 2. Learning Guides

Practical educational content.

Examples:

* AI for Students
* AI for Teachers
* AI for Professionals

Purpose:

Support AI literacy.

Priority:

High

---

## 3. Tool Guides

Tool-focused content.

Examples:

* ChatGPT Guide
* Gemini Guide
* Claude Guide
* NotebookLM Guide

Purpose:

Increase practical AI adoption.

Priority:

High

---

## 4. Safety Content

Risk awareness and protection.

Examples:

* Deepfakes
* AI Scams
* Privacy Risks

Purpose:

Build trust and differentiation.

Priority:

High

---

## 5. Growth Content

Career and business content.

Examples:

* AI Careers
* AI Skills
* AI Certifications

Purpose:

Enable personal and professional growth.

Priority:

Medium

---

## 6. AI News

News and industry updates.

Purpose:

Keep audience informed.

Priority:

Supportive

# Content Repurposing Strategy

One article should create multiple assets.

Example:

Cornerstone Article
↓
Summary Article
↓
Infographic
↓
Newsletter Content
↓
Social Media Posts
↓
Pragya Knowledge Asset

Goal:

Create once, distribute many times.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Every article should link to:

* Parent topic
* Related topic
* Next learning step

Goal:

Create structured learning journeys.

---

# SEO Strategy

Primary Focus:

Evergreen search traffic.

Examples:

* What is AI?
* How to use ChatGPT?
* AI for Students

Secondary Focus:

Timely AI news.

Goal:

Long-term authority rather than short-term traffic spikes.

---

# AI News Strategy

AI News is important but not the primary focus.

News should:

* Explain significance
* Provide context
* Improve understanding

Avoid:

* Pure announcement articles
* Clickbait headlines
* Low-value news aggregation
