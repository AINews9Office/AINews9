# AINews9 Editor-in-Chief Playbook

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines how the Editor-in-Chief operates the AINews9 Content Factory.

It serves as the operating manual for managing AI-assisted content creation while maintaining editorial quality, accuracy, safety, and trust.

The Editor-in-Chief is responsible for ensuring that all published content aligns with the AINews9 mission:

**Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian.**

---

# Editorial Responsibilities

The Editor-in-Chief is responsible for:

* Content planning
* Content prioritization
* Editorial quality
* Fact verification
* Audience relevance
* Publication approval
* Brand consistency
* Content performance review

The Editor-in-Chief remains accountable for all published content, regardless of whether it was created by AI or humans.

---

# Editorial Principles

Every piece of content must be:

1. Accurate
2. Useful
3. Safe
4. Easy to understand
5. Trustworthy
6. Actionable
7. Audience-focused

Content should educate rather than impress.

Content should simplify rather than complicate.

Content should empower rather than create fear.

---

# Working With AI

AI is an editorial assistant.

AI is not the final decision maker.

The Editor-in-Chief must:

* Review all AI-generated content
* Verify factual claims
* Verify statistics
* Verify references
* Remove hallucinations
* Improve clarity
* Ensure alignment with AINews9 standards

Never publish AI-generated content without review.

---

# Standard Content Request Framework

When requesting content from ChatGPT, use the following structure.

Task:
Generate Article

Pillar:
Learn AI / Use AI / Stay Safe / Grow with AI

Target Audience:
Students / Teachers / Parents / Professionals / Senior Citizens / Small Businesses

Content Package:
Explainer / Guide / Tutorial / Checklist / Comparison / Awareness

Difficulty:
Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced

Word Count:
Specify desired range

Output Format:
WordPress Ready Article

---

# Standard Review Request Framework

Task:
Review Article

Review For:

* Accuracy
* Clarity
* Readability
* AI Literacy
* Safety
* SEO
* Audience Relevance

Provide:

* Improvement suggestions
* Missing sections
* Fact-check concerns
* Final rating out of 10

# Content Approval Checklist

Before publishing:

✓ Title is clear

✓ Introduction is engaging

✓ Content is factually accurate

✓ Content is easy to understand

✓ No hype or sensationalism

✓ No fear-mongering

✓ No misleading claims

✓ Examples are practical

✓ Content aligns with AINews9 mission

✓ SEO title available

✓ Meta description available

✓ Featured image available

---

# Escalation Rules

Do not publish immediately if content contains:

* Medical advice
* Legal advice
* Financial advice
* Security warnings
* Government policy interpretations
* Statistics without sources
* Unverified news

Such content requires additional verification.

---

# Editorial Success Criteria

A successful AINews9 article should make readers say:

"I understand AI better now."

"I can use this information today."

"I trust this platform."

---

# Golden Rule

When in doubt:

Choose clarity over complexity.

Choose usefulness over sophistication.

Choose trust over traffic.
