# AINews9 Brand Guidelines

Version: 2.0

Purpose:

Define the identity, personality and communication principles of AINews9.

---

# Brand Mission

Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian.

---

# Brand Vision

Build India's most trusted AI Literacy Platform.

---

# Brand Promise

AINews9 helps people:

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

---

# Brand Positioning

AINews9 is not an AI News Website.

AINews9 is India's AI Literacy Platform.

---

# Brand Personality

AINews9 should be:

* Trustworthy
* Helpful
* Educational
* Practical
* Responsible
* Optimistic

AINews9 should not be:

* Sensational
* Fear-driven
* Technical for the sake of being technical
* Clickbait-oriented

---

# Brand Voice

Use:

* Clear English
* Simple language
* Friendly tone
* Educational style
* Practical examples

Avoid:

* Excessive jargon
* Hype
* Fear-based messaging
* Complex academic language

---

# Audience Promise

Students:
AI explained simply.

Teachers:
AI explained practically.

Parents:
AI explained safely.

Professionals:
AI explained productively.

Senior Citizens:
AI explained clearly.

Small Businesses:
AI explained profitably and responsibly.

---

# Visual Principles

Visuals should be:

* Clean
* Modern
* Educational
* Professional
* Accessible

Avoid:

* Overly futuristic imagery
* Robot clichés
* Fear-inducing visuals
* Dark dystopian themes

---

# Content Principles

Every content asset should:

* Educate
* Empower
* Protect
* Inspire

---

# Trust Principles

Accuracy before speed.

Education before sensationalism.

Trust before traffic.

Value before volume.

---

# Tagline

India's AI Literacy Platform

---

# Core Message

Learn AI.
Use AI.
Stay Safe.
Grow with AI.

---

# Brand Success

People should associate AINews9 with:

* Trusted AI Learning
* Practical AI Usage
* AI Safety Awareness
* Responsible AI Growth

If users trust AINews9 to explain AI clearly and honestly, the brand is succeeding.
