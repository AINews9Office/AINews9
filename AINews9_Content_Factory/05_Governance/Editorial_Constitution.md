# AINews9 Editorial Constitution

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundational Document

Last Updated: June 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines the editorial principles, values, and long-term commitments of AINews9.

It serves as the highest governing document for all content created, published, curated, or distributed by AINews9.

All editorial decisions, content strategies, publishing plans, AI workflows, and future platform features must align with this constitution.

---

# Who We Are

AINews9 is India's AI Literacy Platform.

We exist to make AI Simple, Useful and Safe for Every Indian.

Our goal is not merely to report on artificial intelligence.

Our goal is to help people understand AI, use AI responsibly, protect themselves from AI-related risks, and grow with AI.

---

# Our Mission

Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian.

---

# Our Vision

Build India's most trusted AI Literacy Platform helping people:

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

---

# Who We Serve

AINews9 is built for everyday people.

Our primary audiences are:

* Students
* Teachers
* Parents
* Professionals
* Senior Citizens
* Small Businesses

We prioritize their needs above industry hype, technical complexity, or short-term trends.

---

# Our Editorial Promise

Every piece of content published on AINews9 should help users do at least one of the following:

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

If content does not contribute to one of these outcomes, it should not be published.

---

# What We Believe

## AI Should Be Understandable

Artificial Intelligence should not be limited to researchers, engineers, or technology experts.

Everyone should be able to understand AI regardless of age, profession, education level, or technical background.

---

## AI Should Be Useful

AI should solve real problems.

We prioritize practical applications and real-world benefits over technical jargon and theoretical complexity.

---

## AI Should Be Safe

AI literacy includes understanding risks.

We believe users should be educated about:

* Deepfakes
* AI scams
* Privacy risks
* Misinformation
* Security concerns
* Ethical implications

---

## AI Should Create Opportunity

AI should empower people.

We help individuals, professionals, students, and businesses discover new opportunities created by AI.

---

# Editorial Principles

## Accuracy Before Speed

We prioritize accuracy over publishing first.

Being correct is more important than being early.

---

## Education Before Sensationalism

We prioritize learning over clicks.

We do not publish content solely because it is trending.

---

## Simplicity Before Complexity

We explain concepts using clear and accessible language.

We avoid unnecessary jargon.

---

## Value Before Volume

More content is not necessarily better content.

We prioritize usefulness over quantity.

---

## Trust Before Traffic

Traffic can be earned.

Trust must be protected.

Editorial decisions should never compromise user trust.

---

# Content Priorities

AINews9 prioritizes content in the following order:

1. Learn AI
2. Use AI
3. Stay Safe
4. Grow with AI
5. AI News

News supports the platform.

AI Literacy defines the platform.

---

# AI-Assisted Content Policy

AI is a tool, not the author.

AI may assist with:

* Research
* Draft generation
* Summarization
* SEO optimization
* Image creation

Human review remains mandatory before publication.

AINews9 remains accountable for all published content.

---

# What We Will Not Become

AINews9 will not become:

* A clickbait website
* A rumor website
* A hype-driven AI publication
* A purely technical research publication
* A platform that prioritizes traffic over trust
* A platform that promotes fear, misinformation, or manipulation

---

# Success Definition

Success is not measured solely by page views.

Success is measured by:

* Improved AI literacy
* Increased safe AI usage
* User trust
* Practical impact
* Long-term audience value

---

# Long-Term Goal

Transform AINews9 from an AI News Website into India's Most Trusted AI Literacy Platform.

Every article, guide, image, workflow, feature, and future product should contribute toward this goal.

---

# Editorial Decision Test

Before publishing any content, ask:

Does this help people:

* Learn AI?
* Use AI?
* Stay Safe?
* Grow with AI?

If the answer is no, do not publish it.
