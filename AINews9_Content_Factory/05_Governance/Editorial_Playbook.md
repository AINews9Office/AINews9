# AINews9 Editorial_Playbook

## Purpose

This document defines the content creation, editorial, SEO and publishing strategy for AINews9.

All content published on AINews9 must align with this strategy.

---

# Mission

Making AI Simple, Useful & Safe for Every Indian.

---

# Vision

Build India's most trusted AI Literacy Platform helping people:

* Learn AI
* Use AI
* Stay Safe
* Grow with AI

---

# Strategic Positioning

AINews9 is NOT primarily an AI News website.

AINews9 is an AI Literacy Platform.

News is important but serves as a supporting content stream.

The primary objective is helping people understand, use and benefit from AI safely.

# Editorial Principles

Every article must:

* Be factually verified
* Use simple English
* Avoid unnecessary jargon
* Include examples
* Include FAQs
* Include practical takeaways
* Include Indian context where relevant
* Be useful for beginners

---

# AI Content Policy

AI may assist with:

* Research
* Draft generation
* SEO optimization
* Image generation

Human review is mandatory before publishing.
