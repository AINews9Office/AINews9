# AINews9 Content Packages

Version: 2.0

Status: Operational Standard

Purpose:

Define the standardized content assets generated from a single approved article.

The objective is to maximize the value, reusability and longevity of every content creation effort.

---

# Core Principle

Create Once.

Distribute Many Times.

Learn Many Times.

A single approved article should generate publishing assets, distribution assets, knowledge assets and archival assets.

---

# Content Production Model

AINews9 follows an:

Article
↓
Article Artifact Set
↓
Multi-Channel Distribution

model.

No cornerstone article should exist as a standalone article.

Every cornerstone article must produce a complete Article Artifact Set.

---

# Article Artifact Set

Every approved article generates four mandatory deliverables.

## Deliverable 1

### English WordPress Page

File:

<ArticleID>_EN_WordPress.md

Purpose:

Direct publication to the English website.

Contains:

* Title
* Excerpt
* Featured Image Reference
* Full English Article
* FAQ
* Internal Links
* Call To Action

---

## Deliverable 2

### Hindi WordPress Page

File:

<ArticleID>_HI_WordPress.md

Purpose:

Direct publication to the Hindi website.

Contains:

* Hindi Title
* Hindi Excerpt
* Featured Image Reference
* Full Hindi Article
* Hindi FAQ
* Internal Links
* Call To Action

---

## Deliverable 3

### Master Content Package

File:

<ArticleID>_Content_Package.md

Purpose:

Single Source of Truth.

Used for:

* Archival
* Updates
* Maintenance
* Knowledge Extraction
* Future Automation

Contains:

* Metadata
* Editorial Package
* SEO Package
* Image Package
* Distribution Package
* Knowledge Package
* Review Report
* Publishing Status
* Version History

---

## Deliverable 4

### Image Package

Files:

* <ArticleID>_Image_Brief.md
* Featured Image
* Social Image (Optional)

Purpose:

Maintain reusable visual assets and image regeneration capability.

Contains:

* Image Prompts
* English Text
* Hindi Text
* Visual Concepts
* Branding Instructions
* Regeneration Metadata
