Use:

* Clear English
* Short paragraphs
* Simple explanations

* Educational tone

* Excessive jargon
* Sensationalism
* Fear-based language
* Overly academic writing

# Success Criteria

# Localization Requirement

Every Cornerstone Article must support:

* English Publication
* Hindi Publication

Hindi content should be generated from the approved English Knowledge Master.

Translation should preserve meaning rather than perform literal word-for-word conversion.

The Hindi version must be natural, reader-friendly and suitable for Indian audiences.

After reading the article, the user should be able to:

* Understand the topic
* Explain the topic
* Apply the knowledge
* Take the next learning step
