# AINews9 Production Log

Version: 2.0

Purpose:

Track the production, packaging, publishing and maintenance status of all AINews9 Article Artifact Sets.

This log serves as the operational dashboard for the AINews9 Content Factory.

---

# Status Definitions

## Planned

Topic approved but production not started.

## In Progress

One or more artifacts are being created.

## Review

Waiting for editorial or Hindi review.

## Ready

All artifacts completed and approved.

## Published

English and Hindi versions published.

## Updated

Published content revised after release.

## Archived

Retired from active maintenance.
